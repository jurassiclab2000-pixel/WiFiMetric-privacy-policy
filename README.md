# WifiMetric Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application handles user information.

### Data Collection and Use We do not collect any personal identity information. However, this app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
  * AdMob: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your Advertising ID, device information, and location data to provide personalized ads.

### Third-Party Privacy Policies You can find the privacy policy of the service providers used by the app here:
  * [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
  * [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

### Opt-out You can opt-out of interest-based advertising through your Android device settings (Google Settings > Ads).
