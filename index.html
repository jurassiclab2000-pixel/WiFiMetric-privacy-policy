Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

Welcome to WiFi Metric. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information in our Android application.

1. Information Collection

WiFi Metric does not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or address).

However, we use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect and use certain data, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID).
  • IP address (to determine general location/country).
  • Log data and app usage statistics related to ad interactions.

2. GDPR & International Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK:

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on your Consent provided via the in-app consent form.
  • Data Transfers: Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States by Google.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, delete, or withdraw consent for data processing at any time via your device settings.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected by third-party services (AdMob) is used to:

  • Deliver personalized or non-personalized advertisements.
  • Improve app performance and analyze ad effectiveness.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the advertising ecosystem.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

5. Opt-Out Options

You can opt-out of personalized ads by:

  • Device Settings: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Delete Advertising ID.
  • Web: Visit Google My Ad Center.
